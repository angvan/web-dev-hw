Haiku Senryu Artwork

Migrating flocks of
sandhill cranes meet at river
bugling their joy

The tangled branches
revealed a wonder of life
we're all connected

Canopy of leaves
creates sun-dappled grass and
looks like fallen stars

Uprooted dreams left
to wither on fallow soil
Indian summer

Bluebird, fluttering
at the window, admiring
his own reflection
